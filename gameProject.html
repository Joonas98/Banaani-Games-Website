SHOWCASE (WIP)

The basic concept of the game is fairly generic wave based first-person zombie shooter with some roguelike elements.

What seperates my game from other's will be high amount of different abilities, weapons, upgrades and other features that allow player to have tons of differents to try out.

Also the theme, setting and graphic style of my game is not very serious or realistic, but rather funny and unserious. Terrain and models are low-poly style. SFX and VFX are somewhat cartoony as well.
