SAM PROJECT

2022 - UI / UX Design and Implementation

SAM is short for Smart Autonomous Mobility. SAM is a big project from Metropolia University of Applied sciences along with some other similar schools.

SAM aims to innovate and develop vehicles to level 4 or even level 5 of autonomous driving. To simplify, this means there will be no driving wheel or pedals and driver does not need to do anything

I made my engineer's thesis for this project and I was hired to continue afterwards. For the most part, I worked on the UI / UX for an application that test how many autonomous vehicles one supervisor and supervise. In addition my UI design could work as a base for a real supervising application, as there aren't similar designs openly available.

Nords and Swords

2020 - Lead Programmer

Nords and Swords was a game project with programmers / developers from Metropolia University of Applied Sciences and artists from Stadin AO Vocational College.

The game was inspired from flash game series called Swords and Sandals. The player fights opponents in a turn based combat. Fighting grants the player experience and money to upgrade their attributes and equipment

I was the lead programmer. I ensured that everything gets developed by someone, and I picked up "leftovers" myself, such as menus, scene handling, camera movement, audio implementation etc.
